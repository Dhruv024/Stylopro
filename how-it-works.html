How Stylo Works

Step 01

Analysis & Extraction

Our proprietary computer vision pipeline dissects over 40 distinct body markers to create a precise digital blueprint of your profile.

Step 02

Neural Fitting

Advanced neural networks simulate how specific fabrics drape and react to your unique posture and body shape in real-time.

Step 03

Virtual Try-On

Instantly overlay thousands of catalog items onto your digital twin to see exactly how you'll look before you buy.

Step 04

Fabric Physics

Our physics engine calculates weight, stretch, and light reflectivity to provide a photorealistic representation of high-end materials.

Step 05

AI Learning

The more you interact, the better our AI understands your aesthetic, tailoring future recommendations to your evolving style DNA.
